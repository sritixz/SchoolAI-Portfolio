Solve for x:

x² - 4 = 0

celebration

Correct! Well done!

The square root of 4 is both 2 and -2, therefore x² = 4 leads to x = ±2. Remember to always consider both positive and negative roots for quadratic equations.

Always isolate the squared term first before taking the square root.
